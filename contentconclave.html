About

Content Conclave is a fun event aimed at introducing Content as a Product. Eminent social personalities like Tech Vlogger, Moto Vlogger, Marvel Studio Creatives, Travel Vlogger, Food Vlogger and many more. The Creators will share their journey and also advise the budding creators while answering their doubts. This live interactive session will cover topics like Vlogging, Tools of Social Media Marketing, Algorithms of the Social Media , Strategies and more. The content that they produce will bring eternity to life. This event trains the students through a series of sessions that focus on creatively promoting companies and goods. During the pandemic, we have seen a mainstream growth in digital marketing with many products finding their audience online. The event comprises many activities on promotion of brands in an unconventional way.
